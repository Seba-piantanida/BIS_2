EFFEBIQUATTRO Milano is a leading company in the production of interior doors . Thanks to its ability to interpret the present and a skilful vision of the future, has evolved the boundaries and has flanked the production of doors with the production of interior solutions for the integral customisation of the home. The evolution, innovation and quality that have always characterised the company have been enriched by the inspirations suggested by the creative direction of Matteo Thun & Antonio Rodriguez . With an in-house production of 52,000 sqm and over 10,000 sqm of warehouse space, has been an expression of MADE IN ITALY for over 45 years .
