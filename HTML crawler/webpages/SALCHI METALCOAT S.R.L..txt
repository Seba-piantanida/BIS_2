We are specialized in the production of coatings for Metal Packaging, Caps & Closures, Aluminum Tubes & Monobloc, Coil and Foil.

Since the very beginning Salchi has been a family-owned company. Our history starts in 1993 as a joint venture between the German company Rhenania, a subsidiary of Altana, and Salchi S.p.A. At that time, the coil and packaging coatings activities of Salchi S.p.A. were transferred into the newly established Salchi Rhenacoat. In 2004 Rhenania has sold its shares to the Italian partner, which is renamed Salchi Metalcoat.
Today our production is based in Burago di Molgora, near Milan (Italy) where we passionately work every day thanks to our 180 employees.

Although our roots are Italian, we have a strong international presence, and we bring the experiences we gather around the world to our customers, to help them become real market differentiators, because their success is our success.
