The philosophy of the company is based on 100% commitment to client satisfaction in all aspects, such as high technical specifications of products, excellent design, as well as the most important aspect – high quality of service and standards.
The company, based in Colnago, in the province of Milan, Italy has all the most essential resources – including a fully equipped internal laboratory and technical office, production and quality control department and automated warehouse all of which are staffed by qualified and skilled commercial and administrative staff.
Some years ago, at the outset of LED technology, Quattrobi invested resources in developing LED products, which have now become a major part of their manufactured products.  Combining it with true Italian design, Quattrobi became a respected and leading lighting brand, recognizable in the Retail market not only in Italy but worldwide.
A big advantage for clients is company's capability to manufacture custom made products, which can subsequently be included in the company catalogue.  Quattrobi products have been tested by international authorized laboratories and have gained a number of important certified standards for example CE, UL, GOST, CCC.
