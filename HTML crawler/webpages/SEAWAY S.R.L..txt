steel products by sea. The sea. Our partner, our work, our passion. Seaway is a company registered and dedicated to the logistics of steel products. It operates in all equipped ports in Italy and abroad,  by handling, shifting and storing these materials. Seaway has its main office in Milan and operations branch in Marghera, La Spezia, Ravenna, Marina di Carrara and Trieste. The Company has been established by Mr. Gian Pietro Alberti in 1984, who runs it still now and who has been working in the field of logistics since 1962. The ”core business” of Seaway has always looked towards the logistics in the steel trade by sea, both in import and export.
