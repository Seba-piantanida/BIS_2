Chiudi That same Arḉelik, by the way, to whom he had already sold the Turkish factory in 20201, also formerly Indesit. How does this emphasis reconcile with Whirlpool's own statement that its production site in Lipetsk, Russia, will continue to produce household appliances under the Indesit, Hotpoint and Stinol brands even after the deal is concluded? Unrest in Italian factories The unions reacted immediately, for some time in turmoil dealing with an executive staff who, on the instructions of the top of the Corporation, does not respond to the pressing requests for clarification. And it is proclaimed state of agitation in the various establishments for 8 and 16 July . A state of turmoil that dates back to when the high-end washing machine plant in Naples was closed in an inglorious and controversial way where, among other things, a few months earlier, the production of Aqualtis washing machines had been transferred from the Indesit Comunanza plants in the Marches. That same plant that sent high-quality washing machines to Germany with the high-end German brand Bauknecht (owned by Whirlpool), as well as to the US market. Before reporting in full the answers that Whirlpool Emea sent us, via Maurizio David Sberna, Corporate Communications, CSR and Reputation Director, EMEA at Whirlpool Corporation, we recall that Whirlpool had announced, with a press release dated April 25, a strategic review of its presence in Europe, the Middle East and Africa – destined to face the difficulties deriving from the war in Ukraine and to sales decidedly below forecasts. The strategic review - we wrote then - had in reality been underway for some time, with divestments and closures of sites and brands and, let us add now, far from the forward-looking David R. Witwam, the CEO who in 1989 signed the acquisition of Ignis-Philips from Philips and who had a profoundly ethical vision of the social role of the company, within the framework of a profitable internationalization of the group. Globalization is in retreat, indeed, it is producing ever more evident damage and Whirlpool itself has declared that it wants to concentrate above all on its activities in North America and according to many this does not bode well for European factories…. “We live in a less global world, where the general tensions on the supply chain have fully exploded during the covid period and continue to create disruption on production and supply. On this, Whirlpool is trying to respond with the greatest possible agility both by managing production and its flexibility in an integrated way (e.g. stopping when certain components are missing and reorganizing more quickly when raw materials are available). In this, the trade unions have been in recent years in Europe and in Italy a partner with whom to work in synergy and from whom we have received support". What choices closely concern Whirlpool Italy? “We are rapidly adapting business to a world that is gradually becoming less globalised. Last year, for example, Whirlpool reported in Siena a production line of chest freezers which was previously located in China, and which serves the North American market. Availability of a site in a geographically strategic point (proximity to ports and logistics areas such as Livorno/Civitavecchia), production capacity that can be optimized and a boom in demand have allowed an operation which – throughout 2021 – has also seen the hiring of 60 temporary and the return to full productive hours. The line also brought with it around €5 million of investments in 2020-21". A few years ago you transferred administrative employees to a specially created company, Whirlpool srl, separating it from manufacturing personnel. And this, as has happened in other situations, is considered as one of the ways of "lightening" the manufacture, making it more attractive for potential buyers..... “In 2022 we completed the last step of the corporate integration of Indesit. These are normal corporate organization practices, which in the last 6 years have seen several changes in structure (from Indesit Company to Whirlpool Europe, then to Whirlpool EMEA and Whirlpool Italy, then to Whirlpool Management). These are normal corporate structure operations that have nothing to do with presumed restructuring plans or anything else. Rather, in Italy Whirlpool invested 2015 million between 2021 and 837 of Euros, and is investing 87 in 2022 alone between product, process, innovation and technology. since the beginning of the year, 180 permanent employees have been hired at the Cassinetta site alone and another 100 are being hired this month. In consumer service, among other things, we are the only ones to have a completely internalized center which – from Fabriano – manages all post-sales management with over 110 permanent operators. In general, I would say that all these data demonstrate in my opinion that Whirlpool is still firmly established in EMEA and that the trend of market share growth is a further reinforcement of the effectiveness of the strategy implemented". congiunto rientri in una discussione più ampia di conferma di tutti gli impegni precedenti» FABRIANO, 11 febbraio 2022 – Con comunicazione del 31 gennaio scorso, Whirlpool EMEA ha  annunciato  la  cession di ramo d’azienda a favore della nuova costituita Whirlpool Management SRL . L’operazione, che sarà effettiva dal primo aprile, riguarderà complessivamente  1107 dipendenti delle sedi impiegatizie italiane, di cui circa 350 nell’area fabrianese. Durante l’esame congiunto svoltosi con le  Organizzazioni  sindacali  nazionali  e territoriali e le  RSU  facenti  parte  del  Coordinamento,  a  seguito  di  specifiche domande, la  Multinazionale  ha  affermato  e  garantito  che  non  ci  sarà  alcuna ricaduta  per  le  persone sulle  condizioni  di  lavoro  e  retributive,  sull’anzianità   di servizio tanto meno sulla sede di lavoro. «Si tratterebbe quindi di un’operazione esclusivamente  societaria che – commenta la segreteria Fiom di Ancona – portando in Whirlpool Mangement tutte le  funzioni  che  non  sono  connesse  con  la  produzione e con la distribuzione dei prodotti, dovrebbe condurre ad una semplificazione e ad una razionalizzazione  della  struttura». «Sarà pertanto nostro impegno  vigilare  in  maniera  costante  sulle  ricadute  concrete  che tale operazione potrebbe avere sulla condizione delle persone – prosegue la Fiom – quindi si rende indispensabile definire  chiaramente  i  perimetri  dell’operazione ed   assicurarsi   del fatto  che  nel  trasferimento  di  funzioni,  nessuno  resti  escluso,   anche   chi   ad   oggi ancora     non      ha      una      posizione      effettiva      di      lavoro      dentro      l’azienda. In questa logica abbiamo avanzato la richiesta che l’esame congiunto rientri in una discussione più ampia di conferma di tutti gli impegni precedenti chiedendo con determinazione che venga convocato al più presto, e comunque prima della conclusione della procedura,      uno      specifico      incontro      per      approfondire      le      missioni produttive    e    le    prospettive    di    tutti    gli    stabilimenti    e    degli    uffici». «Confermando   l’esclusiva   natura   societaria   dell’operazione   ed   escludendo   comunque la possibilità di eventuali  esuberi  che  ne  scaturirebbero – queste le ultime parole della nota Fiom – la Whirlpool  ha  affermato che  ad  oggi  non  esiste  un  nuovo  piano  industriale ma  si  è data  disponibile  a riprendere un’interlocuzione con le parti sociali per un’informativa nazionale che sarà calendarizzata nei prossimi giorni ».
