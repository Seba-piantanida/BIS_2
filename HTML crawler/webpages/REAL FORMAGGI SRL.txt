We cater to merchants, street vendors, canteens, restaurants, pizzerias and communities, offering a wide range of dairy products, cheeses, cured meats and a selection of food including pasta, rice, oil and wine. Besides operating as a traditional wholesaler, we are also present in Seregno with a cash & carry at Corso Matteotti 146. At this sales outlet specialising in cheeses and cured meats, customers can personally choose from a very large quantity of items, assisted by our highly qualified staff. Our offer includes typical local, regional, PDO, PGI and also foreign products. Our History A long commercial tradition selling cheeses and cured meats: Real Formaggi has been active since 1972. This ensures high expertise in the dairy sector, which is put at the service of customers, enabling them to buy quality products at affordable prices. Quality, expertise and convenience have been the guiding principles of our many years in business. We boast a vast assortment of traditional Italian cheeses and cured meats, including Grana Padano, Parmigiano Reggiano and Parma Ham. We also offer the finest specialities from all regions of Italy, from Pecorino to Toma and Caciotta, products that are both fresh and aged; cured meats from Veneto, Calabria and Tuscany; and internationally renowned foreign products like Swiss Emmental, Edam and Maasdam. We operate on an area of 2000 m2 equipped with cold rooms, a warehouse for ageing Grana Padano and Parmigiano Reggiano, robots for cleaning cheese moulds, and a room for vacuum-packing portioned cheeses. Our company is EEC 03/650 certified, a certification guaranteeing compliance with EU hygiene rules. We have vehicles equipped with refrigerators, plus ATP and health certificates necessary for transporting foodstuffs at the correct temperatures. Shop/restaurant/hotel/canteen managers and street vendors can obtain supplies via this site or directly from our cash & carry, choosing products from a vast range of top-brand cured meats and cheeses. The automatic weighing and vacuum-packing system also allows customers to purchase small pieces that meet every need. Our staff offer their technical expertise to advise you on what to buy.
