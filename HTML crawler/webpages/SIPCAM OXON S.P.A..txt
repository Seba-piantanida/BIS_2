SIPCAM OXON has started from Italy in1946 following its origins and has emerged at global level focusing on product development, production in both synthesis and formulation, and the ability to find original solutions.

Today the Group's activities span across the whole world, with subsidiaries in more than a dozen countries, from Europe to the Americas and from Africa to Australia.
