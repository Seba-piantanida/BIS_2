Our history Eagleburgmann Italia S.r.l., in the beginning known as Burgmann, has been present in the Italian market since the the 1970's; initially through an agency, however, a direct subsidiary was opened in 1989. From the beginning, the growth has been constant and promising and, since 2000, the company also took over the distribution and service of expansion joints (fabric, rubber and steel) from KE, a Danish company that has already been part of the Burgmann group. In 2003, the German Freudenberg Group acquired the Burgmann Group and, from the following year, also the Burgmann-BT S.p.A. (founded in Vicenza in 1966 from the company Boribello & Testa SAS , and then acquired by the German company Burgmann Dichtungswerke GmbH in 1998). In 2005, with Freudenberg already active partnerships with the Japanese group EKK, owner of Eagle, it was decided to merge the Eagle and Burgmann groups and thus, the Italian subsidiary became EagleBurgmann Italia S.r.l.. In 2008, to improve the company's presence across the Italian territory, EagleBurgmann Italia S.r.l. opened a new Service Center, with commercial offices, in Ravenna and in 2015 a further investment was made with the opening of the Catania Service Center. In 2019, EagleBurgmann Italia S.r.l. merged with Burgmann-BT S.p.A., which in 2013 changed its name to Eagleburgmann BT S.p.A.. Then, a production reality with over 50 years of history has been integrated. Between the end of 2020 and the beginning of 2021, the company expanded the range of offered services, including predictive and lubrication activities within the broader program of reliable support called "MR3P" (Monitornig Resolution Repair Reliability Program). The fascination of sealing technology The development of sealing technology is closely related to industrial progress. Then-Today-Tomorrow: Nearly all machines and systems need to be sealed reliably and securely – either dynamically or statically. Seals are consequently relevant to function and safety and assume the role of a key component in industrial plants. Impegno per un futuro migliore, per l’ambiente e l’economia. Maggiori informazioni Rely on excellence Tenute eMG per pompe L’eccellenza è il nostro standard: attraverso materiali unici e ad alte prestazioni. Il design ottimizzato del soffietto ed il disco brevettato in PTFE rendono le tenute eMG ancora meglio di prima. La nuova generazione è compatibile al 100% con la precedente serie MG e stabilisce nuovi standard nel campo delle tenute a soffietto elastomerico per pompe. Maggiori informazioni Rely on excellence RoTechBooster – la migliore protezione per prestazioni di livello superiore Tecnologia all'avanguardia sviluppata da EagleBurgmann: i RoTechBooster ad azionamento elettrico garantiscono prestazioni di tenuta di prima categoria in condizioni operative variabili. Il tuo compressore sarà quindi perfettamente attrezzato per ogni situazione. Maggiori informazioni Rely on excellence Giunti di espansione per ogni applicazione Come elementi di collegamento flessibili nei vani di aereazione, nei pozzi di scarico e nei sistemi di tubazione, i giunti di espansione hanno il compito di compensare le oscillazioni di pressione e di temperatura, le vibrazioni e gli spostamenti nei punti di collegamento.
