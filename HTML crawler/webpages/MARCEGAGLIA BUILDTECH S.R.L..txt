The company’s expertise and in-depth know-how, acquired over the years, enables it to offer the market a wide range of high-tech finished products . Marcegaglia Buildtech, with headquarters in Milan, is structured as three distinct company divisions , each focusing in a specific product category; metal building envelope : with insulated panels for roofing, walls, sectional doors and corrugated sheets; construction equipment : tailor-made solutions for scaffolding and construction; guardrails : steel safety barriers. Marcegaglia Buildtech’s production is carried out at two dedicated plants in Northern Italy. Marcegaglia Buildtech also has a widespread presence in Italy and abroad through a dealer/agency network .
