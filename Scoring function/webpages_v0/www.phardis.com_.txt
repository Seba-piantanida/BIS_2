COMPANY PROFILE
Phardis provides a logistic outsourcing service for medicines, cosmetics and health products.
Set up in the 90s, the company has developed the know-how and skills needed to hold a key role in the medicine logistic distribution segment. An authorised pharmaceutical workshop operates alongside its highly specialised storage, packaging and management services. Care for the end customer does not respond to a purely commercial logic, but aims to safeguard a consumer who, as a patient, needs greater protection than any other category. Keeping a constant eye on regulatory novelties, the company is in the forefront in its search for sustainable solutions related to the entire sector's regulatory evolution.
