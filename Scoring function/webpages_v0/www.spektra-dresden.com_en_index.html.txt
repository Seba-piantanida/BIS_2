Applications Overview
Applications Overview
SPEKTRA offers specialized services and coordinated product solutions for diverse industries. Tailored to the needs of individual branches of business, industry, development and research.
Discover now how we can support you.
Condition Monitoring
Condition Monitoring
Condition monitoring is an important module for safety and efficiency. SPEKTRA provides these products and services for different applications in condition monitoring.
Environmental & immission protection
Environmental & immission protection
The protection against harmful effects on humans and their environment caused by noise and vibrations is an acutely important topic. We enable you to implement protection and monitoring measures in compliance with standards.
Crash test & automotive industry
Crash test & automotive industry
Precision means safety. For accurate sensor verification for use in crash tests and in the automotive industry, we offer effective complete solutions, diverse exciters and components and a wide range of accessories.
Aviation & aerospace
Aviation & aerospace
Regular ground vibration tests, structural analyses for condition monitoring and sensor tests in the development phase are typical examples of countless application scenarios for test and measurement technology in the aerospace industry.
Vibration Test Systems
Vibration Test Systems
Your high-performance vibration test systems need to be checked and calibrated regularly. We offer tailored solutions and DAkkS-accredited services to minimize downtime.
Motor vehicle monitoring
Motor vehicle monitoring
Adapter for general inspections, decelerometers, and acoustic measuring equipment from the everyday life of motor vehicle monitoring
Seismology
Seismology
From assisting in the development of new seismometers to certified calibration and structural excitation for seismological research to equipping entire laboratories.
Sensor & measuring device manufacturer
Sensor & measuring device manufacturer
Developers and manufacturer of sensors or measuring instruments find in SPEKTRA a competent partner for metrological investigations, calibrations, sensor characterizations and consulting from the development process to series production.
Universities & research
Universities & research
Cost-optimized portfolio for modal excitation, NVH and characterization tailored to universities and research institutions, as well as expert support in research.
Calibration service providers
Calibration service providers
Turnkey systems consisting of modular hardware and software components, assistance with laboratory equipment and other additional services specifically for calibration service providers and laboratories.
National Metrology Institutes
National Metrology Institutes
Primary and secondary calibration systems as well as individual ­components such as high-quality exciters for NMI. Together with numerous national institutes for metrology, SPEKTRA ensures comparability of measurement results worldwide.
