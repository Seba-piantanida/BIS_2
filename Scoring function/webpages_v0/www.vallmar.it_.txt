MATERIALS
Discover the wide range of sheets and blocks that we work
Vallmar is always on the lookout for perfection and quality, choosing its own materials rigorously and diligently, coming from the main caves in all the world. Marbles with splendid veins and nuance, granite which is resistant and practical for both external and internal use, stone among which are valuable sandstone and slate with varied finishings and technical characteristics, as well as onyx and precious stones representing some examples of a vast portfolio for the customer.
With hundreds of articles in sheets or blocks of various dimensions, Vallmar is able to satisfy the most demanding requests in architectural studios, well-known interior designs and furnishing companies as well as personal customers.
Shaping the material on the basis of its purpose thereby keeping the beauty unaltered and respecting the natural characteristics, the company carries out standardized or ad hoc projects, embellished with skill, internal and external settings.

MORFOSI
Furniture and objects where design and tradition merge
Il brand Morfosi si pone come linea guida la reinterpretazione stilistica del marmo, attraverso la fusione con altri materiali nobili. Trasformazione è la parola chiave di questa azienda, in cui la materia prima, il marmo, diventa mobili e oggetti dalle linee semplici, ma di grande effetto visivo.
The Morfosi brand has as its guideline the stylistic reinterpretation of marble, through the fusion with other noble materials. Transformation is the key word of this company, where the raw material, marble, becomes furniture and objects with simple lines, but with a great visual effect.
