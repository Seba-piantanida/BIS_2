TCL Communication Technology Holdings Limited (TCT), with its North America headquarters based in Irvine, California, is a wholly owned company of TCL Corporation, a global consumer electronics brand with products currently sold in over 160 countries. With a mobile handset product portfolio that includes devices from Alcatel and TCL, TCT is currently the fifth largest handset manufacturer in North America. The company also operates nine R&D centers worldwide and employs more than 13,500 people globally.
