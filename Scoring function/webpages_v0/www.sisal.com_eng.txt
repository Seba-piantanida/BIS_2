Innovation
We constantly invest in the implementation of innovative ecosystems to encourage the adoption of cutting-edge technological solutions. This is why we have opened our first Innovation Lab in Turin: a new technological center of excellence promoted entirely by the Company and two digital hubs in Albania and Turkey manned by strong technological skills
Responsible Gaming
The Responsible Gaming Program is an important pillar of our strategy to guarantee the protection of customers, in particular the most vulnerable groups, through information initiatives, prevention of problematic gaming phenomena, assistance for critical issues related to gaming. We have always conducted and supported research and studies to analyze and monitor perception and sensitivity on these issues.
sustainability
Our sustainability path has evolved over the years in line with national and international best practices to respond to stakeholder expectations in respect of the environment and the highest standards of business ethics and governance. Three goals to be achieved by 2030: Zero problem gamblers, Zero gender pay gap, Zero net CO2 emissions.

Responsible Gaming
Our Responsible Gaming Program guarantees the safeguard of our customers, specifically more vulnerable users, through information initiatives, problem gaming prevention schemes and education around Responsible Gaming behaviour and gaming-related risks.
