Logicom Distribution Italy
Who we are
Logicom Italy began operations in 2005 through its office located in the northern outskirts of Milan. Since its launch, Logicom Italia has added major Technology brands such as Intel, Microsoft, Kingston, Corsair, Lenovo, MSI, and many others, to its product portfolio, and offers their complete range of products throughout the Italian market. The company now has reach that covers the entire geography in Italy, and aims to increase its product breadth to include other market-leading brands as well as to satisfy its customers and develop a strong and dedicated channel of resellers.
