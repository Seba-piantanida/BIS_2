Since Pivi S.r.l. began its activity has always dealt with the engineering and manufacturing of custom-designed power transformers. Throughout the decades we have gathered experience in all special applications of power transformers, becoming, in both dry-type and liquid-filled transformers, an internationally recognized premium supplier to all major customers. ABOUT US
Our firm has its roots in an historical Milan-based transformer company, founded in 1946 by the Pivi brothers, which has always dealt with the engineering and manufacturing of custom-designed power transformers. Over the years we have gathered experience in all special applications of power transformers, becoming an internationally recognized premium supplier to all major customers. In the 1985 the firm name became Specialtrasfo and continued developing products, making the original business grow both organically and by acquisitions of niche-specialized transformer manufacturers. We are committed to continuously improving the range and quality of our products, investing in human resources, technology, engineering and infrastructures.

OUR PRODUCTS
✓ Rectifier Transformers
✓ Converter Transformers
✓ Power Transformers
✓ Furnace Transformers
✓ Testing & Regulating

Our goal is to provide its customers with the best quality products and services available on the market through continuous development, improvement of design engineering and technology of our products and continuous investments in all our three production facilities.

INNOVATION & QUALITY
Specialtrasfo’s rigorous quality control policy covers every design and production stage. We are ISO 9001:2008 certified and we invest in our internal quality control system. Our products are designed, manufactured and tested according IEC and IEEE/ANSI standards.

SERVICE AND ASSISTANCE
We provide support and assistance on transformers of any brand, from standard maintenance to major repairs. We have our own instruments and equipment ready to be dispatched worldwide and selected local partners so that we can quickly reach our customers.
