Istituto Ricerche Applicate was founded in 1983 as an applied research laboratory, setting itself the ambitious vision of identifying and developing game-changing ideas and transforming scientific innovation into better healthcare solutions. 

Committed to delivering innovative solutions, I.R.A. is divided into 3 operating segments: active ingredients and raw materials for the cosmetic industry, contract manufacturing of finished cosmetic formulations and production of CE 0373 certified Medical Devices of class I, II and III. 

The courage to pursue innovations evolved into one of the most comprehensive product portfolios in the market, featuring a wide range of class III injectable Medical Devices with linear and cross linked hyaluronic acid, providing a range of treatment options of the highest quality with the highest safety standards both in the aesthetic and orthopedic fields.

In a world where patients always expect better results, relying on high levels of purity, low concentration of nucleic acids and proteins, and an extremely low endotoxin level (≤ 0,5 EU/ml) – significantly lower than European Pharmacopoeia limits for injectable products – together with an extremely sophisticated purification process, I.R.A. takes HA injections to a whole new level. 

I.R.A. offers a range of minimally invasive aesthetics dermal fillers, enabling the achievement of completely natural and long-lasting results. Our multifaceted portfolio of formulations at various concentrations enables a tailor made treatment upon each patient’s individual need. 
Our injectable Medical Devices also cover the orthopedic area of viscosupplementation, offering a complete and complementary line of treatments for the management of inflammatory type pain.
