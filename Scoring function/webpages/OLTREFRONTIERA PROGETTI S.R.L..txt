Segui Visualizza tutti i dipendenti (34) Segnala azienda Chi siamo Founded in 1993, OLTREFRONTIERA PROGETTI Srl is a dynamic and highly motivated company operating as a producer of window displays, tools, pop-up stores, shops and projects for companies in the Luxury and Sportswear Fashion segment.

Our esteemed clients include Giorgio Armani S.p.A., Kering S.A, LVMH S.E, Moncler S.p.A., Prada Group, VF International, OTB Group and Compagnie Financière Richemont SA.

Our company has strategically positioned itself in four locations:

The primary company, established in 1993, is located in Italy, in the heart of Brianza, where our headquarters are based.

The second company, established in 2005, is based in Guangzhou, China, in the Guangdong "Factory of the World" region.

The third company, established in 2017, is based in New York City, USA.

The fourth company, established in 2018, is based in Paris, France.

Since 2023, we have expanded our presence to Dubai.

Our international team of designers and architects creates innovative concepts. We consider budget, timing, production capacities, quality, packaging samples and logistics to be crucial variables during prototype development to ensure end products meet our clients' real needs.

With an extensive network of suppliers, we possess unlimited production capacity, managing multiple productions simultaneously in Italy, China and Mexico. This allows us to offer the best quality products at competitive prices.

The presence of Oltrefrontiera Progetti in the Far East and Mexico significantly reduces shipping costs to locations outside Europe.

Our team tracks and monitors the entire business cycle, including administration, planning, production, quality control, packing, storage and logistics.

OLTREFRONTIERA PROGETTI Srl holds ISO 9001, SA8000, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, FSC, ECOVADIS, RINA Sustainability Manager, GRI, DL 231 and A&O certifications. GuangZhou Oltrefrontiera Trading Co. Ltd is ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and SA8000 certified, as well as being FSC certified.
